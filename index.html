Maple Adventure

what we do

who we are

We are the union for outdoor sports, specialized in tailored holidays for groups and individuals. We also offer services for groups, schools, and enterprises who look for a weekend or several days of activities in nature.

what we do

Canyoning

Pass through the most famous canyons of western Serbia, which are widely known for their beauty and the experience it offers.

Abseiling

Try yourself in one of the most extreme activities that maple-adventure offers, and that is Abseiling. With our best and most experienced guides you will experience an amazing experience that will never be forgotten.

Sup & Kayak

Stand Up Paddleboarding is the hottest new water sport and an exciting way to spend an active day on the river. SUP trips are recommended for any adventure seeker looking for an exciting workout or just a thrilling way to spend the day.

Zipline

One of the most interesting activities that maple-adventure offers is ziplines, for both children and adults.

Viewpoint

Visit the most beautiful and best viewpoint of western Serbia with our best team and you will have an unforgettable experience.
